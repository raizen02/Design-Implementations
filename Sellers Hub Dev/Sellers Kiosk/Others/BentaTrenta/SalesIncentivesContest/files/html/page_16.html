1. The coverage period of all 2014 contests and incentives for
sellers and brokers is from December 1, 2013 to November 30, 2014,
except for the Monthly Incentive, which will start on January 1,
2014.
- The Quarterly Contest shall commence every fiscal quarter
from December 2013 to August 2014.
- The Semi-Annual Contest (1st semester) shall cover all
qualified sales from December 1, 2013 to May 31, 2014.
2. All sales must have initial requirements (IREQ) and complete
documentary requirements (CREQ) within 60 days from booking
date.
- For projects without LTS, only CREQ and required PDCs must
be submitted. Signed documents are no longer required.
